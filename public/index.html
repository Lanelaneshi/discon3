~Welcome to the Walk Together Game~

We live long enough to know that:
so much of those relationships between matters are not Either/Or,
they are more so Both/And.

Dissonance

Dissonance is a 2 person game exploring the dynamic on the synchronicity of walking together. In this vast cosmic reality, walking alone will amplify noise in your head. So accompany the other company (you might or might not like), and keep yourself sane and practiced.

Consonance

Consonance is a 2 person game finding out the beauty of watering a flower! When flower gets water, both player will enjoy music generated out of that happiness. If you don't water the flower, then the whole world turns into silence.
